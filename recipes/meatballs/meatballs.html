Meatballs

Ingredients

  • 4 lbs ground beef
  • 2 lbs ground pork
  • 1 egg per pound of meat
  • Old stale bread soaked in warm water (2 to 3 cups worth)
  • 3 cloves garlic finely chopped per pound of meat (18 cloves altogether!)
  • 1 to 1.5 cups of chopped Italian parsley
  • Lots of salt and pepper

Preparation

  1. Mix together in large bowl
  2. Shape into meatballs by hand
  3. Bake at 400 until just brown
  4. Add these to the gravy/sauce when simmering to enhance the flavor
